Safaga


Safaga, also known as Port Safaga, is a town in Egypt, on the coast of the Red Sea, located 53 km south of Hurghada . This small port is also a tourist area that consists of several bungalows and rest houses, including the Safaga Hotel, with a capacity of 48 rooms.
