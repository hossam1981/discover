Hurghada tryOut


Hurghada is a beach resort town stretching some 40km along Egypt’s Red Sea coast. It’s renowned for scuba diving, and has numerous dive shops and schools in itsmodern Sekalla district. There are many restaurants, bars and nightclubs, whilethe old town, El Dahar, is home to traditional Egyptian coffee shops and souks.Hurghada’s long stretch of sandy beach is lined with resort hotels.
