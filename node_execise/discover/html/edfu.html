Edfu


The city of Edfo is located 132 k.m south of Luxour,The city of edfo was the capital of the second Nome in Upper Egypt since the time of Narmer the Uniter of the two lands and the founder of the first dynasty, The city now belongs to Aswan, The ancient name was Gba, daba, taba, atbo, edfo,The ancient name of the city is referring to the triumph of hours over his uncle Seth which maybe took place her in the city of edfo The main god of the city is hours of Bhdt which was the religious name of the city or a part of it, always hours of bhdt is represented in the form of the solar-disc with the two wings.
