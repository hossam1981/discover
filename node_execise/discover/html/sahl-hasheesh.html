Sahl El Hasheesh


Sahl Hasheesh ,is a bay located on the Red Sea coast of Egypt, Sahl Hasheesh is a bay located on the Red Sea coast of Egypt, across from Sharm El Sheik, approximately 18 km south of Hurghada International Airport.
